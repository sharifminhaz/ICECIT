Scope

ICECIT 2020 invites papers from all areas of electrical, electronics, information and communication engineering. The topics of interest include but are not limited to:

  • Track 1: VLSI, Embedded Systems and Nanotechnology

    • • VLSI Design and Fabrication
    • • Embedded Systems and Applications
    • • Internet of Things
    • • Power Electronics and Drives
    • • Nanoelectronics and Nanotechnology

  • Track 2: Communication Technologies

    • • Wireless and Optical Communication Systems
    • • Microwave and Radio Frequency
    • • Satellite and Space Communications
    • • Smart Grid Communications
    • • Power Line Communications
    • • Green Communication Systems
    • • MIMO Systems and Networks
    • • THz Communication Systems
    • • 5G and 6G Communication Devices and Techniques
    • • Communication QOS, Reliability and Modeling

  • Track 3: Networks and Security

    • • Computer Networks and Systems
    • • Optical Networks and Systems
    • • Cognitive Radio Networks
    • • Ad hoc and Sensor Networks
    • • Next Generation Networks
    • • Network & Cyber Security and Privacy

  • Track 4: Networks and Security

    • • Computer Networks and Systems
    • • Optical Networks and Systems
    • • Cognitive Radio Networks
    • • Ad hoc and Sensor Networks
    • • Next Generation Networks
    • • Network & Cyber Security and Privacy
